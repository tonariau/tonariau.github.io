Atmosfera

Branding and UX/UI project created for Atmosfera platform by Hub Rocket, whose goal is to be a space for information and knowledge exchange.

The design was based on Rocket's website, with adaptations inspired by blogging platforms. The visual aspect is predominantly minimalist and focused on text content. To set up the interface, the 8pt grid method was used, while the components were created using the Atomic Design methodology.

The typographic family and color palette considers the platform's personality and legibility, along with Hub Rocket's institutional design. The background images bring immersion to the spatial concept, with halftone textures that gives personality to the website and avoids conflicts between the background and the content. The identity icons represent, respectively, a star, a galaxy, and the atmosphere itself, and they can be used as graphics.
