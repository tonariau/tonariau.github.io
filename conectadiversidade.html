Conecta Diversidade

Conecta Diversidade is a boutique consultancy specialized on DEI (Diversity, Equity, and Inclusion), that renders services of training, talks, events, and communication focused on subjects related to race, mental health, disabled people, LGBTQIA+, women, and etarism.

It seeks to communicate the amplitude of its work in an objective, outstanding, and minimalist way through its visual identity. The idea was not to limit the visual to any specific niche on DEI field, while it could not be generic or ambiguous. For that, it was created a geometric meaningful logotype, that conveys connection and diversity.

With vibrant colors, balanced typography, and simple compositions, the identity reflects the values of the brand, as well as the accessibility of its content. Each semantic code was thought out and tested to guarantee legibility and contrast. The visual identity represents the size of the company and the quality of its service.
